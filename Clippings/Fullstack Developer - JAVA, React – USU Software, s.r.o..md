Introductory performance

We are looking for a Full-stack developer for a team that develops our product for IT Cloud Monitoring.

USU products, which we have been developing for 25 years, are used by users in 187 countries around the world. We have over 1200 customers across industries like banking/insurance, retail, industry, services, or state institutions

Job offer

## You are our man if:

- You enjoy programming frontend in modern framework
- Writing code in Java + Spring Boot is not a problem for you
- You can also control frontend technologies - React
- You can communicate with your surroundings in English
- You like to learn new things
- You have experience with SQL, JPA, microservices in Docker- and Kubernetes
- You enjoy agile development

## What awaits you here?

- Development of a modern Java application used to monitor the state of infrastructure
- Development of Java application where you can expect a lot of challenges and tasks
- Lots of work on our frontend in React
- Using technologies such as Docker, Kubernetes, SpringBoot, OpenTemetres,...
- Work in a stable company, where there is an informal environment and a friendly atmosphere

## What do we offer?

- Contract for an indefinite period
- Flexible working hours and the possibility of partial home office (max 12 days per month), workplace Brno or Prostějov
- Contribution to meals in the form of a flat-rate meal voucher
- 25 days of leave and for every 2 years worked in the company an extra 1 day up to 30 days
- Online teaching of English and NJ during working hours
- In order to enjoy the day off to the fullest, you will get points to the Cafeteria, which you can use, among other things, for leisure activities such as entrances to the sports ground or cinema, education, books, massages, flexi passes or pensions, medical facilities or internet allowance
- Corporate events (Christmas party even for partners in a nice hotel with a sleepover, USU summit and other teambuildings...)
- Corporate Interest-Free Loan
- Possibility to rent a company car
- Pleasant offices, scented coffee from modern coffee machines, vegetable and fruit days.... it's just a day with us.
