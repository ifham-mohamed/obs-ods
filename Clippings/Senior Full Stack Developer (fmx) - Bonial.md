## Job description

**bonial helps you save time, money and paper!**

We are a digital advertising partner for offline businesses - we support retailers in their marketing activities and help them find a new audience. **Would you like to join and be a part of digitalisation of retail?**

bonial is part of **Axel Springer SE** and is the leading "Drive to Store" partner in Germany and France. Every day we inspire millions of users by connecting them to their favourite shops and brands via our platforms " **[kaufDA](https://www.kaufda.de/)** " and " **[MeinProspekt](https://www.meinprospekt.de/)** " in Germany, and “ **[bonial](https://www.bonial.fr/webapp/)** ” in France.

bonial welcomes both **full- and part-timers!**

![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jeDC_b64Cw0)

**As a Senior Full Stack Engineer in our team, you will be:**

- Playing a key role in the development, maintenance, and integration of our systems.
- Working closely with other teams across the Client Facing Platform product area.
- Designing cloud infrastructure in AWS using technologies such as Terraform and CloudFormation.
- Applying your experience programming in Java, Python, and JavaScript (TypeScript).
- Developing user interfaces using React to create responsive and dynamic web applications.
- Working with technologies like Spring Boot, Docker, Kubernetes, Camunda, AWS, Terraform, REST APIs, etc.
- Building systems in the spirit of Agile methodologies (Scrum, Kanban).

## Job requirements

**What you should bring:**

- A passion for full stack development.
- Solid experience with Java and microservices architecture.
- Experience with Python (or a willingness to learn it).
- Proficiency in JavaScript (TypeScript) and experience with React.
- Proficient experience with Spring Boot, JUnit, Maven, JPA, PostgreSQL, REST APIs, etc.
- Understanding of Docker, and knowledge or interest in Kubernetes.
- Background in working in an agile software development team.
- Independence, dedication, and ability to deliver production-ready (and clean) code.
- The ability to work independently, responsibly, accurately, and reliably—with a data-driven mindset.
- The spirit of a team member, discussing solutions and ideas in a positive and respectful way—we strive for knowledge sharing.
- Fluency in English (German is not required).
	**Nice to have:**
	- Experience with AI technologies.
	- Experience with AWS (or other cloud computing platforms).
	- Experience with Event-Driven Architecture.
	- Experience with Kubernetes.
	- Experience with messaging services (e.g., Amazon SNS, SQS).

This role offers the opportunity to work on impactful projects that contribute to environmental sustainability while utilizing a modern technology stack. If you are a motivated engineer looking to make a difference, we would love to hear from you!
