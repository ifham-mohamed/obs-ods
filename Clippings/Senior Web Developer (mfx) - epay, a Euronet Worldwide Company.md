## Requirements

**Do what you love:**

- You will work on challenging and diverse projects and accompany the entire software development life cycle, because we map all steps in-house.
- You are developing our core component library based on Vue.js and the related web applications based on the Nuxt framework.
- As part of an agile team, you are co-responsible for the architecture of our various web applications.
- You are working on our APIs in cooperation with our Java developers.
- You connect microservices and content from the CMS Magnolia.
- You support our legacy framework by building some templates and tweaking some CSS.
- You support other web developer colleagues as a coach.

**Show what you can:**

- You have 5 or more years experience ideally in the payment / gift card / fin tech industry.
- You have at least 3+ years of experience in:
- Vue.JS version 2 and version 3 and migrating Vue applications to version 3
	- Server-side rendering with Nuxt version 2 and 3
	- Typescript, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS, SCSS
	- Bootstrap & Tailwind
	- REST API
	- Designing web applications & writing concepts using modern architecture patterns
	- Developing mobile apps with capacitor and IONIC framework would be a plus
- You worked in the past with
- IntelliJ, VS Code, Docker & Kubernetes, Git/GitHub, CI / CD, decoupled Content Management Systems (CMS), e.g. Magnolia, Typo3, etc.
	- Jira & Confluence
- You bring excellent problem solving and multitasking skills and have a logical and pragmatic attitude.
- You love to work in an agile team and support each other when working towards the sprint goal.
- You have excellent communications skills and can work independently.
