Do you want to build your career in a prestigious software-consulting company? Do you want to participate in unique projects and work with state-of-the-art technologies? Do you control React at a median or senior level? Maybe we’re looking for you to strengthen our development team.

**WHAT WILL BE YOUR TASK?**

- cooperation on the development of SW solutions for our important clients
- development of the assigned part of the application with React technology according to the specified specification or other assignment
- implementation of application functions, change requirements, testing of created functions, correction of errors, deployment into production
- cooperation and communication with other members of the development team in the implementation of the solution

**WHAT DO WE EXPECT?**

- University education of informatics or economic specialization
- experience with React-em, typescript at the senior level, respectively at least median (minimum 2 years of experience with active work in the technology)
- knowledge of the design patterns used in technology and their application
- knowledge of integration patterns and microservice architecture (Swagger, Json, Rest API...)
- experience with the integration of React FE on back-end systems (Java/Kotlin...)
- Independence and a Proactive Approach
- communicativeness and team spirit
- Pleasant and professional demeanor
