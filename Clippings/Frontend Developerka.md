## Frontend Developer

Become part of a team that will work on new products and functionalities that will be unique on the Czech and Central European market! We want to give the new team the opportunity to create truly unique projects in the unique combination of RegioJet and Student Agency brands.

[Reply](https://regiojet.jobs.cz/detail-pozice/odpovedni-formular?r=reply&id=2000600514&rps=233&impressionId=7bdfbd4a-955d-4c65-8096-b9bb5f8a04f8)

**What awaits you?**

- Development and improvement of features and applications in Next.js, which are used daily by our customers and internal teams.
- Active monitoring of the latest trends and technologies in development and their integration into our projects.
- Participation in code review and quality of code in the team.

**What do we expect from you?**  
  

- You can encode using HTML and CSS.
- You Know Typescript, React, Next.js.
- You can use Git.
- You have an overview of the new FE development technologies.
- You have good analytical and critical thinking.
- Your knowledge of Tailwind will help.
- You can not do without excellent knowledge of Czech or Slovak, we use English only for programming.

**What can you look forward to?**

- Opportunity to be for new projects that will become a challenger to the entire market in Central Europe.
- A friendly team consisting of specialized experts with top know-how in the field of digitization.
- Flexible working hours.
- Offices in the center of Brno.
- Employee benefits (Multi-sports card, discounts on company products, 5 extra days off, loyalty rewards, internal education).
