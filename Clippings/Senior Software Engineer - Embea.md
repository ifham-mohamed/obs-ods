## Job description

**THE COMPANY**

Embea is rebuilding life insurance based on current technology. Instead of going through brokers, you get insured in your favourite app or website. Get covered in seconds, thanks to our one-click checkout. Our vision is to get insurance out of the way, so people can enjoy life without worries.  

  

To support bootstrapping of our insurance system, we are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our engineering team. An ideal candidate for us is a self-starter, that can proactively identify areas for system\\infra improvements and act as a stakeholder for such changes.

**Salary range**: €75,000 - €100,000  

  

**YOUR MISSION**

- Be a founding member of our engineering team, design and develop our insurance company system from scratch
- Participate in team discussions, bring ideas to the table, and write clean, testable, maintainable, and performant code
- Bring best practices to the table and apply the latest technology to ensure an excellent end product
- Apply DevOps/NoOps principles in our infrastructure-as-a-code environment

## Job requirements

**WHAT YOU NEED TO SUCCEED**

- Production experience with Kotlin and/or Java
- Deep understanding of service-oriented and microservices architectural patterns, troubleshooting methods, and best practices
- Experience with AWS (ObjectDB, Lambda, CloudWatch, EC2, SQS, SNS, Cognito)
- Experience working with DevOps (Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform)
- Problem-solving and collaborative mindset
- Good written and verbal communication skills in English; other languages are a plus

  

**NICE TO HAVE**

- Experience in Insurance/FinTech
- Experience and willingness to work with frontend technologies(React, TypeScript)
- Mentorship experience: the capability to successfully transfer knowledge

  

**WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU**

- Flexible work hours, the freedom to set your own schedule
- Hybrid Working Environment: Real office or home office? You decide! You should be able to come to our Berlin office from time to time
- Go on a retreat: Become a digital nomad for a whole month once per year and work remotely from any location
- A company culture based on mutual trust and respect
- A structured onboarding plan for your successful start
- A diverse, non-ideological work environment that welcomes everyone
- A personal development budget of €1,000 per year
- Free choice of laptop and other hardware
- Free choice between a 30 and 40 hours contract
- A relocation package with visa support for those who need it
