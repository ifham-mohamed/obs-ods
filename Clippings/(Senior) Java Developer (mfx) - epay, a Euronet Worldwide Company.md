Benefits & Incentives for over 20 years: **cadooz** develops high-quality, exciting product solutions for business customers - from DAX companies to small businesses. With over 160 employees in Hamburg and Munich, we offer bonus & loyalty programs, benefit worlds, vouchers & non-cash rewards for new customer acquisition, customer loyalty, employee motivation and much more.![](https://workablehr.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/photos/591413/c6811ab15d397e32fffd3e379c77759b.png) **cadooz is part of Euronet Worldwide and epay**, the experts for digital & physical prepaid products worldwide.

## Requirements

**Do what you love:**

- You will work on challenging and diverse projects and accompany the entire software development life cycle.
- As part of an agile team, you are co-responsible for the architecture of our various Java applications.
- You are working on our APIs in cooperation with our web and front-end developers.
- You support our Junior Java developers as a coach and support them to grow.

**Show what you can:**

- You have 7 or more years of experience in the payment / gift card / fin tech industry.
- You have great skills with
- Java, Java EE, Spring Boot
	- SOAP and REST API
	- MySQL or Maria DB
- In the past you worked with
- IntelliJ, VS Code, Docker & Kubernetes, Git/GitHub, CI / CD, Decoupled CMS (Magnolia)
	- Jira & Confluence
- You bring excellent problem solving and multitasking skills and have a logical and pragmatic attitude.
- You love to work in an agile team and support each other when working towards the sprint goal.
- You have excellent communications skills and can work independently.
