- Proficient in **Java** and related tools and technologies (Ruby on Rails, Node)

- Ability to apply software development best practices and principles on a daily basis
- Experience with the full software development lifecycle.
- Ability to utilise expertise and experience to reach a high level of productivity
- Fluent English, written and spoken
- High level of motivation and eager to learn
