# Law

<mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">Define the concept of “Law” ?</mark>

Law is the terms defined as rules made for proper regulations of society or correct conduct in life. Further "LAW"  denotes rule and principles, enforced by parliament  to control of regulated people's behavior, with the view of standard justice, peaceful living, and society securing.

- **Law may be defined as the means by, which is behaviors of human in societies is regulated**
- **Law may be defined as the means by, body of rule which is spells out the right & Duties of the various sectors in society**

<mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">Nature of the Law</mark>

Historically society ha no "LAW" to govern conduct, but instead relied in the force of custom, religion, social pressure.

No one is above the law. Every one is obey and confirm to law. in order to obey the law, it is necessary to have some understanding of the Law

> Ignorance of the law is not an excuse to break the law 
> [[Legal Systems -Law lesson  1.pdf#page=4&selection=61,0,63,23|Legal Systems -Law lesson  1, page 4]]


The need for a knowledge of the law cannot be over – emphasized . In modern times , almost all human activity is affected by the law in one way or another .

- **When you are born , the law requires , that your birth be registered , and when you die the law requires a death certificate before you can be buried or cremated . Further, no newspaper will normally agree to publish an obituary notice unless the death certificate of the deceased person is produced .** 

- **The law requires that any person who wishers to drive a vehicle must be over a certain age (18 years) and possess a valid driving license . Under our elections law , the law requires a person to be 18 years and above to be qualified as a voter . These are only a few examples to show to you how the law has become an integral part of our life . Thus as shown above, “Law” is an integral part of any society. No civilized society can function without law.** [Explain why law is important for the proper functioning of a society , and how it impacts on the lives of all citizens in a society][[Legal Systems -Law lesson  1.pdf#page=3&selection=7,1,10,54|Legal Systems -Law lesson  1, page 3]]
